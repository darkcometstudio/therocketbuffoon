The Rocket Buffoon

Help this fine statesman with a jetpack to avoid cunning obstacles to get back into his beautiful country, from the continent of Mexico. Skill game, the goal is to collect the most available points. Any resemblance to actual persons, living or dead, or actual events is purely coincidental.

Goal of the game: Reveal the cunning obstacles, and collect the most possible points.
